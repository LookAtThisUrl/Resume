Projects – Social Icons

I have created icons for social networking websites (more or less) in a vector format.
The icons come in dark grey (#444444), light grey (#808080), and blue (#009BCD). This collection currently has icons for Twitter, App.net, Facebook, Google+, Dribbble, Instagram, 500px, Flickr, YouTube, iTunes, GitHub, Fancy, emails, a cross, and downloads.
